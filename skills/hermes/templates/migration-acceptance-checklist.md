### Aim and purpose of the document

The migration acceptance checklist describes the review points and criteria that are necessary for the migration acceptance decision.
> _Adjustments (e.g. additions, deletions) to the review points are at the discretion of the project management. A few pointers:_
> _Evaluation: 'Yes' means that the criterion is relevant and has been fulfilled, or that the outcomes document has been created and approved. A 'No' means not relevant or the criterion is not (yet) met. If the answer is 'No', an explanation may be necessary._
> _Responsible: a role responsible for the audit_
> _If 'Yes' is selected, the date corresponds to the fulfilment date of the criterion. If 'No', the date on which the review point was last assessed_
