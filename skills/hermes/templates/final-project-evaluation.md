### Aim and purpose of the document

The final project evaluation forms the basis for the project closure decision. It gives the project sponsor information on the target/actual comparison with regard to the project and procedure objectives in terms of deliverables, scheduling, and finances. The content of the lessons learned outcome is documented in a summary. The content and deadlines for project success monitoring are set.
