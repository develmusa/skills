### Aim and purpose of the document

The legal basis analysis describes the legal basis intended for the project outcome and the possible need for it to be amended. Special attention is paid to the communal, cantonal, national, and - where applicable - international legal as well as product-relevant and regulatory specifications that the envisaged solution, including its effects on the peripheral systems, must comply with (product compliance).

## Gaps identified
> _Based on a comparison of the existing and any changing legal basis and the relevant rough requirements, any gaps to be filled with a legislative project are to be identified. In doing so, it should be noted that a project also may be reinterpreted and expanded in the course of its execution, and this may not be covered by the existing legal basis. It may be worth making provision for such a case in good time so that the deployment of the new solution is not delayed by the lack of a legal basis._
